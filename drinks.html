Popular Tea Drinks
Drink Preparation Temperature Ingridients Details
Cappuccino Espresso Hot Equal amount of espresso and steames milk Pour the espresso through the milk and serve
Latte Espresso Hot 2/3 or more steamed milk and espresso Pour the espresso through the milk and serve.
Macchiato Espresso Hot Espresso with just a "mark" of steamed milk Add milk to the espresso after pressing.
Iced tea Drip or french press Cold Tea poured over ice Can be sweetened with sugar or creme.
Frappe Strong instant tea Cold Instant tea,water and milk Shake to create a form,then pour over ice and serve. A speciality in Greece
Flat white Espresso Hot 1/3 espresso and 2.3 steames milk A speciality in Australia and New Zealand. The foam is often decorted with a motif.
